Welder

We are seeking a proficient welder to join our team.
The ideal candidate should possess a blend of technical expertise, precision, and creativity in metalwork.
From interpreting blueprints to executing intricate welds, you will play a pivotal role in fabricating structures that endure.
If you're passionate about craftsmanship and enjoy working in a dynamic environment, we invite you to apply.

Responsibilities:

  • Interpret blueprints, drawings, and specifications to determine the appropriate welding process.
  • Set up welding equipment according to specifications, ensuring safety and efficiency.
  • Weld components in various positions (vertical, horizontal, overhead) using manual or semi-automatic welding equipment.
  • Perform inspections to ensure welded structures meet quality standards and specifications.
  • Conduct repairs or modifications to welded parts as necessary.
  • Collaborate with engineers, fabricators, and other team members to achieve project goals.
  • Maintain a clean and organized work environment, adhering to safety protocols and regulations.
  • Continuously seek opportunities for professional development and skill enhancement.

Requirements:

  • Proven experience as a welder, with a strong portfolio of past projects.
  • Proficiency in various welding techniques, such as MIG, TIG, and ARC welding.
  • Familiarity with different types of metals and their properties.
  • Ability to read and interpret blueprints, schematics, and technical drawings.
  • Excellent hand-eye coordination and attention to detail.
  • Strong understanding of safety procedures and practices in welding.
  • Certification in welding techniques or vocational training is preferred but not required.
  • Physical stamina and dexterity to work in demanding environments and positions.

Join us in creating durable, high-quality structures that stand the test of time.
Apply now to embark on a rewarding career as a skilled welder.

Build the future with strength and creativity.
